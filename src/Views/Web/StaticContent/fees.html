PRIVATE LESSONS

Private lessons with any coach are 20 or 40 minutes in length and instruct students specifically and individually about technique, balance, tactical progressions, rhythm, and coordination. While classes are designed to create proficient fencers, private lessons are a way to gain a deeper understanding of fine points that can make a proficient athlete into a highly skilled one.
